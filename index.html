Top 10 Best Processors

Home GPUs Laptops Monitors

1. AMD Ryzen 9 7950X

16 cores, 32 threads, based on Zen 4 architecture. Outstanding for gaming and productivity.

2. Intel Core i9-13900K

24 cores (8P + 16E), Raptor Lake beast. King of single-core performance.

3. AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D

8 cores, 3D V-Cache, excellent gaming CPU with great power efficiency.

4. Intel Core i7-13700K

Perfect mid-tier chip with 16 cores (8P + 8E) for gaming and editing.

5. AMD Ryzen 5 7600X

Best budget gaming CPU on Zen 4 with great efficiency and thermals.

6. Intel Core i5-13600K

Top value pick with great multithreaded and gaming performance.

7. Apple M2 Max

Incredible performance per watt for creative professionals in MacBooks.

8. AMD Ryzen Threadripper Pro 5995WX

64 cores for extreme workstation loads. Best for 3D rendering and simulations.

9. Intel Core i3-13100F

Best entry-level gaming processor for budget gamers and casual users.

10. AMD Ryzen 5 5600G

Comes with integrated Vega graphics. Great for budget builds without a GPU.
